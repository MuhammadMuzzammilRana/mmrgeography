ANTARCTICA

Antarctica is the fifth largest continent of the World.Here are some keypoints of Antarctica.

Geography of Antarctica

Size: 14 million square km (5.4 million square miles)

Border: Antarctica does not have any international border like other continents.

Regions: divided into several parts including East Antarctica, West Antarctica, Antarctic Peninsula, Interior Antarctica, Coastal Antarctica and Subglacial Antarctica.

Population: about 1,000 in winter and 5,000 in summer.

Countries number: there are 0 countries.
