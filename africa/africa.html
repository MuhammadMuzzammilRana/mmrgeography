AFRICA

africa is the second largest continent in the world, after asia. here are some key details about africa:

geography of africa

size: 30.37 million square km(11.7 millions square miles).

borders: surrounded by the Mediterrian sea, the Suez Canal, Red sea, Indian Ocean and the Atlantic Ocean.

regions: divided into several regions including north Africa, west Africa, east Africa, central Africa and southern Africa

population: 1.3 billion people.

Countries number: there are 54 countries.
