SOUTH AMERICA

South America is the fourth largest continent in the world. Here are some key facts about South America.

Geography of South America.

Size: 17.84 million square km (6.89 million square miles).

Borders: it borders Caribbean Sea, Atlantic Ocean and Pacific Ocean.

Regions: divided into several parts including Andean Regions, Amazon Basin, Brazilian Highlands (Brazilian Plateau), Southern Cone, Gran Chaco, Guianas, Pampas and Patagonia.

Population: 430 million people.

Countries number: there are 12 countries.
